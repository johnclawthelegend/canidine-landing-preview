FAQ

Questions restaurants actually ask.

Do I have to re-type my menu?

No. Upload the PDF, Word doc, spreadsheet, or even a photo of the printed menu. AI parsing extracts dishes, descriptions, and likely allergens — you just review and approve.

What if an allergen gets flagged wrong?

Our state-of-the-art allergy engine cross-references your dishes against millions of ingredient records, and you approve everything before it goes live. Guests always see your cross-contact disclaimer, and anything uncertain is labeled "confirm with staff" — never silently marked safe.

Does this replace my POS or menu system?

No — it sits alongside whatever you use today. Your printed menu stays; the QR simply gives allergy-aware guests a safe way to read it.

Is this a medical guarantee?

No — it's an operational clarity layer. You stay the source of truth: your recipes, your oils, your cross-contact policy, published clearly. Guests get a restaurant-approved starting point instead of a guessing game, and your staff get fewer vague allergy conversations.

What happens after the founding window closes?

Founding members keep their rate for the first 12 monthly billing cycles. New signups after September 24, 2026 pay standard pricing.
